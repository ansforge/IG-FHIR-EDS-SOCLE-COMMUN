Logical Model: Person OMOP Table

Official URL: https://interop.esante.gouv.fr/ig/fhir/eds/StructureDefinition/Person Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2024-07-29 Computable Name: Person

This table serves as the central identity management for all Persons in the database. It contains records that uniquely identify each person or patient, and some demographic information.

Usage:

  • Refer to this Logical Model Profile: Condition Era OMOP Table, Condition Occurrence OMOP Table, Death OMOP Table, Device Exposure OMOP Table...
